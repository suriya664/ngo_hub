Education Program Success Story

Our Education for All program has been making significant strides in rural Maharashtra, transforming the lives of hundreds of children who previously had limited access to quality education.

Through this initiative, we've provided school supplies, textbooks, and educational support to over 500 children across 25 villages. The program focuses not just on providing materials, but also on ensuring that children have the support they need to succeed academically.

Key Achievements

  • 500 children provided with school supplies
  • 25 villages covered across Maharashtra
  • 95% school attendance rate improvement
  • 50 scholarships awarded to meritorious students

One of the most heartening stories from this program is that of Priya, a 15-year-old girl who became the first in her family to go to college thanks to the scholarship she received through our program.

We're grateful to all our donors and volunteers who have made this program possible. Your support is truly making a difference in the lives of these children.
